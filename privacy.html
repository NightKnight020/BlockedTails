Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Data We Collect

BlockedTails does not collect, store, or process any personal information. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services.

Flight Data

All flight data displayed is sourced from ADS-B Exchange, a public aggregator of ADS-B transponder signals. This data is publicly broadcast by aircraft and is not private.

Third-Party Services

The site uses Cloudflare for hosting and CDN services. Cloudflare may log request metadata per their own privacy policy.
