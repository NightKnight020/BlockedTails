What are Blocked Tails?

Some aircraft owners request that their registration (tail number) be blocked from public flight tracking databases like FlightAware and FlightRadar24. This is often done by government agencies, military contractors, wealthy individuals, or corporations who want privacy.

However, ADS-B Exchange does not honor these blocking requests — their receivers capture raw transponder signals and publish all data. This means blocked aircraft are still visible here.

Common blocked registrations include private jets, government aircraft, and military support planes.
