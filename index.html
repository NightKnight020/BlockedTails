What are Blocked Tails?

Learn more ▸

Some aircraft owners request their registration be blocked from FlightAware and FR24. ADS-B Exchange does not honor these requests — it captures raw transponder signals, making blocked aircraft visible here.

Common blocked registrations: government aircraft, military support planes, private jets of public figures.
